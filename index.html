About us

RUA is a Non-Profit Organization that aims to empower people from disadvantaged backgrounds, enabling access to knowledge and culture. We organize theatre plays, debates, and young people gatherings, in order to create a future where everyone, regardless of the street they are born on and their socio-economic background, can thrive and achieve their dreams.

Frequent Asked Questions

How do we create impact with your NGO? 

We Empower Disadvantaged Individuals: Your mission is to empower people from disadvantaged backgrounds by providing access to knowledge, culture, and opportunities, you can help individuals break barriers and overcome socio-economic challenges.

Non-Formal Education: Utilizing non-formal education methods is an effective way to engage and empower individuals. We promote interactive learning experiences that can lead to practical skills and personal development.

Diversity of Backgrounds: The diversity of your members' backgrounds is a valuable asset. This diversity brings a wide range of skills, experiences, and perspectives to your organization, which can be harnessed to create innovative solutions and effective programs.

Community Engagement: We use a hands-on approach to directly impact the lives of the people we serve.

International Experience: Your members' experience in youth exchange projects, training courses, and volunteering not only enriches their own skills but also allows us to connect with a global network of like-minded organizations. This can lead to international partnerships and collaborations that amplify your impact.

Focus on Key Areas: Your focus on financial, health, and digital literacy, as well as exposure to arts and theatre, political engagement, and active participation, covers a broad spectrum of skills and experiences that can help our beneficiaries.

Local and Global Impact: We believe that "changing the world starts on our STREET// RUA". We promote local engagement because we recognize the potential for broader impact on a global scale.

How do you ensure transparency in your organization?

Clear Governance Structure: We have defined roles and responsibilities for board members, staff, and volunteers, ensuring that decision-making processes are transparent and well-documented (we have all our meeting conclusions registered! 

Open Communication: We encourage members to share ideas, concerns, and feedback freely. We hold regular meetings to discuss progress, challenges, and future plans.

Financial Transparency: We maintain financial records and make them accessible to relevant stakeholders. We have annual financial reports, including income and expenditure statements, balance sheets, and audits. We explain, for every project, how funds are allocated and used.

Program Impact Reporting: We provide regular updates on the impact of your programs and initiatives. In our social media channels, we share success stories, testimonials, and data to demonstrate how your organization is achieving its mission and goals.

Open Records: We maintain accessible records of organizational policies, bylaws, meeting minutes, and other important documents. We also make these documents available to members and the public upon request (just ask using our contact form!)

Data Protection: We handle personal data and sensitive information with care and in compliance with data protection laws. We inform stakeholders and partners about your data privacy practices and how their information is used and protected.

Transparency in Decision-Making: Clearly communicate how decisions are made within the organization, especially those related to resource allocation, project selection, and hiring processes. Involve relevant stakeholders in decision-making when appropriate.

Annual Impact Reports: We create annual impact reports summarizing your organization's achievements, challenges, and financial performance. We share these reports with stakeholders.

Who we are?

Associação da Minha Rua is a Non-Profit Organisation - NGO  created by a group of people from different parts of Portugal but based mainly in Caldas da Rainha. We have all known each other during Erasmus + youth projects, and we have decided to create something together to change people's lives. We believe that changing the world starts in our own street.
